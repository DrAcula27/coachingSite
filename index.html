About Me

Welcome to Rincewind Coaching! I,m Rincewind, a seasoned expert in survival and chaos navigation. With years of experience avoiding calamity and adapting to unpredictable situations, I help clients thrive in the most unexpected of circumstances. Let me show you how to turn running from problems into a path to success.

I am a seasoned survivalist with years of experience navigating chaos, danger, and magical misadventures. As a former wizard of Unseen University, I possess a deep understanding of the unexpected and specializes in helping clients turn calamity into opportunity. I firmly believe that success isn't about mastering everything—it's about running faster than your problems (and sometimes, the people causing them).

Renowned for my quick thinking and adaptability, I have survived countless perilous encounters, from world-ending magical catastrophes to negotiating with hostile sentient luggage. My coaching methods are unconventional yet highly effective for those seeking to embrace unpredictability and achieve success in high-pressure, chaotic environments.

  1. Survival Leadership Coaching

  2. Conflict De-escalation and Diplomacy
    • Strategies for resolving disputes with minimal involvement.
